Welcome to WCNPS 2017!

Workshop on Communication Networks and Power Systems

Paper submission deadline is 29.09.17.

Following the success of the first Edition of the Workshop on Communication Networks and Power Systems, we are pleased to announce the second workshop. WCNPS 2017 will  be held at the University of Brasília, and will count on several plenary talks held by experts and researchers of the industry, the government and the academia. Moreover, the event also includes the presentation of contributed papers, that will undergo peer review in order to provide feedback to the authors and ensure a high-quality program.
